Bonney Lake Red Robin Information Page

You never know where you'll be when the craving for a hot and juicy burger will strike. Maybe you're making copies at work or studying for a math test. Perhaps you're taking in the beauty of Victor Falls City Park or exploring the Washington State History Museum. Wherever you are, there's only one place to go to satisfy your hunger: the Bonney Lake Red Robin. With over 25 Gourmet Burgers to choose from, you'll find your YUMMM® in record time. For the burger purists, we offer our Red Robin Gourmet Cheeseburger, or our Keep It Simple. For those who are looking to add a little spice to their day, may we recommend our Scorpion or the Burnin' Love? Don't forget to check out our fan faves, the Whiskey River® BBQ and our returning MadLove. Paired with our iconic Bottomless Steak Fries®, it's a meal you'll never forget. Pre-game your meal with our Towering Onion Rings® or a 10-inch Edge To Edge® party-cut Donatos® Pizza. Your Bonney Lake Red Robin on 192nd Avenue East also offers amazing entrées, fresh salads, sandwiches and wraps. End your meal on a sweet note with a slice of our Mountain High Mudd Pie, or our hand-made Chocolate Covered Pretzel Gourmet Milkshake. No matter if you're looking to enjoy lunch, dinner or a drink in our bar while you check out the game, Red Robin in Bonney Lake is here with all your YUMMM® needs! We can't wait to see you!
